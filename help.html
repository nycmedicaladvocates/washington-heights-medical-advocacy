Request Free Help

This is a free community service. We help Washington Heights residents navigate healthcare.

Emergency? If someone is in danger or needs immediate medical help, call 911.

What we can help with (for free)

  • Finding a doctor or clinic
  • Help enrolling in NYC Care or other programs
  • Making medical appointments
  • Understanding medical bills or insurance letters
  • Transportation planning for appointments
  • Lowering medication costs
  • Help after a hospital visit
  • Senior support (forms, portals, follow-ups)

We are volunteers. We do not charge money and we do not sell insurance.

Fill out the form below

Tell us what you need help with and a volunteer will contact you. If your situation is urgent, please also call your doctor’s office or clinic directly.

How this works

  1. You fill out the form
  2. A volunteer reviews your request
  3. We contact you by phone or email
  4. We guide you step-by-step on what to do next

We are not doctors and do not give medical advice. We help you navigate the system.
