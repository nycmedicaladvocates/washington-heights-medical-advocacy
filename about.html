ABOUT

Free medical advocacy and navigation in New York City

NYC Medical Advocates is a volunteer program that helps people navigate healthcare and benefits. We focus on practical next steps: where to start, who to contact, what questions to ask, and what documents to prepare.

Free medical advocacy and navigation. No cost.
Request Help Start Here FAQ Privacy

What we help with

  • Finding care and clinic entry points
  • Scheduling and appointment preparation
  • Medical bills and paperwork navigation
  • Medication cost questions and official programs
  • Mental health resource connections
  • Transportation options for appointments

How it works

  1. You request help by form or phone.
  2. A volunteer follows up to clarify the goal.
  3. We outline steps and share reputable resources.
Resource standards

We prioritize official and reputable sources such as NYC.gov, NY State, NYC Health + Hospitals, and widely recognized public benefit and advocacy organizations. Programs, eligibility, and hours can change, so confirm details with the provider.

Safety and disclaimer
In an emergency, call 911. For mental health crisis support, call or text 988. This site provides general information and free navigation support. It is not medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
