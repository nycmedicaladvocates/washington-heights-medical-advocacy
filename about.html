ABOUT

What we are

NYC Medical Advocates is a volunteer program that helps New Yorkers navigate healthcare and benefits. We focus on practical next steps: who to call, where to go, what to ask, and what documents to prepare.

Free medical advocacy and navigation. No fees.
Request Help Privacy FAQ Impact

What we help with

  • Finding care and clinic entry points
  • Scheduling and appointment preparation
  • Medical bills and paperwork navigation
  • Medication cost questions and official programs
  • Mental health resource connections
  • Transportation options for appointments

How it works

  1. Request help by form or phone.
  2. A volunteer follows up and clarifies the goal.
  3. We outline steps and link you to reputable services.

Safety and disclaimer
In an emergency, call 911. For mental health crisis support, call or text 988. This site provides general information and free navigation support. It is not medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
