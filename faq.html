FAQ

Common questions
Is this free?

Yes. There is no fee for navigation support.

What kinds of issues can you help with?

Finding care, scheduling, bills and paperwork navigation, medication cost questions, transportation, and mental health resource connections.

How fast do you respond?

We respond as soon as volunteers are available. If it is urgent or you feel unsafe, call 911 or 988 as appropriate.

Do you help outside NYC?

We focus on NYC resources. We can sometimes point to official statewide resources.

Can you speak Spanish?

Yes. Use the Spanish pages or request help in Spanish.

Last updated: Feb 2026

Safety and disclaimer
In an emergency, call 911. For mental health crisis support, call or text 988. This site provides general information and free navigation support. It is not medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
