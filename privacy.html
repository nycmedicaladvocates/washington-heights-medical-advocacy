PRIVACY

How we handle information

We aim to collect the minimum information needed to provide navigation support. We are a volunteer program and not a medical provider.

100% free community-based support • Not medical advice • Not for emergencies

What we may ask for

  • Contact information (phone number) to follow up
  • A summary of what you need help with
  • Basic details needed to navigate a bill or form (account number, date of service)

Avoid sharing sensitive medical details unless necessary for navigation support.

Your safety

  • Emergency: call 911
  • Mental health crisis: call or text 988

If something feels urgent or unsafe, use emergency services rather than waiting for a response.

Safety notice
Emergency: call 911. Mental health crisis: call or text 988. This site provides general information and navigation support only. It is not medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
