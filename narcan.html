Free Narcan (Naloxone)

If someone may be overdosing: call 911 right away.

What is Narcan?

Naloxone (often called Narcan) can reverse an opioid overdose. It is safe and easy to use.

Always call 911 after giving naloxone, because the person still needs medical care.
